Color Theory

Primary Colors

The primary colors are red, yellow, and blue. They cannot be created by mixing other colors together.

Red
Yellow
Blue

Secondary Colors

The secondary colors are green, orange, and purple. They are created by mixing two primary colors together.

Green
Orange
Purple

Tertiary Colors

The tertiary colors are created by mixing a primary color with a secondary color.

Red-Orange
Yellow-Green
Blue-Violet

Color Harmonies

Analogous

Analogous colors are adjacent to each other on the color wheel.

Yellow-Orange
Red-Orange
Blue-Violet

Triadic

Triadic colors are evenly spaced on the color wheel.

Navy Blue
Sage Green
Warm Gold

Tetradic

Tetradic colors are four colors that are evenly spaced on the color wheel.

Rose Red
Teal Green
Warm Amber
Deep Indigo
